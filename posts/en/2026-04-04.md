## Learnings

### Loan-to-Capital Conversion Is Difficult

Investigated whether a loan could be temporarily booked as a liability and later converted to equity capital. Conclusion: it's not straightforward. Debt (loans) and equity (capital investment) are fundamentally different in nature, so a simple reclassification isn't possible.

### VC Investment Scale

VCs typically need to see a market opportunity in the range of 100 billion yen (roughly $700M+) to consider investing. Smaller markets don't attract venture capital.

### Public Offering Disclosure Rules

When announcing a public offering (soliciting investment) at a venue with 50+ attendees, prior filing is required. This is regulated under the Financial Instruments and Exchange Act.

### Corporation vs. LLC

Two main corporate structures: Kabushiki Kaisha (corporation) and Godo Kaisha (LLC). Corporations are better for attracting external investment; LLCs have lower setup costs. The choice depends on the business direction.
